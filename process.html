The process is easy peasy!

Step 1. Apply through our website for your position of choice.

Learn more about what positions we offer here.

We will review your application and schedule an interview over Skype should you meet our requirements.

Step 2. Attend an online interview via Skype

If we decide you're a good fit for the position, we will send you an Employment Intention Letter and a copy of the sample contract for you to read.

Step 3. Sign the Intention Letter and send it to us alongside all the documents required for your Z-Visa application if you decide to accept our offer.

We will apply for the Work Permit and Invitation Letter from the Government Offices on your behalf, after which you will receive all the documents from us to apply for the Z working visa (needed to legally work in China) from the Chinese Embassy or Chinese Consulate.

Step 4. Apply for your Z-Working Visa from the Chinese embassy or Chinese consulate.

The Z visa should take around 3 – 7 days to process.

Step 6. Now book your flight and prepare to embark on a brand new journey in China!

We'll meet you at the airport and show you to your new apartment.

Welcome to China!
